Calculate Your Green Score

The Green Score is a reflection of your eco-conscious lifestyle. Unlike traditional carbon footprint calculators that primarily focus on negative impacts, the Green Score considers both positive actions and their impact on the environment. For every eco-friendly action you take, you earn Green Credits that contribute to your Green Score, empowering you to understand your impact and strive for a sustainable future.

How is the Green Score Calculated?

The Green Score is derived from a comprehensive assessment of your lifestyle. Here's how it works:

Positive Actions: Actions such as recycling, supporting eco-friendly brands, and conserving water add positive points to your score.

Negative Actions: Activities with higher carbon footprints, like excessive energy usage, will deduct points.

Carbon Offsetting: Engage with offsetting projects to counterbalance your carbon emissions and boost your score.
